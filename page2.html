Kieran Mangla was born on April 28th 2001 in Farmington, Connecticut. From a young age, Mangla enjoyed debating and politics.
Mangla attended Farmington High School from 2015-19, where he served as Class President in 2015-16 as well as Executive President of the Student Council in 2019-20.
Mangla currently attends The College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia where he is studying Political Science
